About Us

Here you will find the best reference for information on Vincent van Gogh's life and work. Over the course of his career, he produced an incredible amount of work: 1,000 drawings, 150 watercolors, ten watercolors, nine lithographs, one etching, and over 900 paintings. We've also compiled his biography, which covers his life from childhood to death, including his mental state, influences, and the world in which he lived. We wish to reach as many people as possible with Van Gogh's legacy and to show his genius the respect it deserves.
